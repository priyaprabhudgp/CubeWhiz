How to Solve Your Cube

  1. Orient your cube: Hold white center facing down, yellow center facing up
  2. Face the green center towards you (Front face)
  3. Input the colors:
    • Top (White) = W
    • Bottom (Yellow) = Y
    • Front (Green) = G
    • Back (Blue) = B
    • Right (Red) = R
    • Left (Orange) = O
  4. Click each face square and select the color you see
  5. Click Submit to solve!
